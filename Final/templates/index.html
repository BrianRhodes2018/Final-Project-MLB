MLB analysis of how certain stats help predict win totals.

The way baseball has viewed statistics has changed over the last 20 or so years. Unlike most major team sports in the USA, baseball is much closer to being a one on one sport. The majority of the time any given play comes down to the pitcher versus the hitter. Where homeruns have always been viewed as the sexy play much like a slam dunk in basketball they do not necessarily lead to wins.
